AGREEMENT
This Agreement is entered into between:
Territorial Tutoring SA (Pty) Ltd (“TT”)
and
_______________________________________ (“Head of Training & Quality” / “HTQ”)
Effective Date: ______________________
1. APPOINTMENT
The Individual is hereby appointed as:
Head of Training & Quality (HTQ)
This role holds system-wide responsibility for TT-OS integrity across all pods and
Territory Directors.
2. ROLE & AUTHORITY
The HTQ is responsible for:
Enforcing TT-OS across all pods
Auditing Territory Directors
Maintaining tutor execution quality at scale
Detecting and eliminating system drift
Training and qualifying new Territory Directors
Acting as final authority on quality-related disputes
The HTQ operates independently and is expected to require zero operational
babysitting.
3. PERFORMANCE STANDARD (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
The HTQ must maintain:
Consistent tutor execution quality across all pods
No systemic quality collapse
Accurate audit enforcement across TD layer
Continuous development of new high-performing TDs
Failure to maintain system integrity constitutes grounds for removal.

4. COMPENSATION STRUCTURE
4.1 PLATFORM OVERRIDE
The HTQ shall receive:
5% of total platform revenue across all active pods
This is performance-linked and scales with system growth.
4.2 SALARY (STAGE-DEPENDENT)
A fixed salary may be introduced based on:
Company stage
Cash flow stability
Salary is not guaranteed and may be adjusted.
5. EQUITY STRUCTURE (PERFORMANCE-BASED)
5.1 TOTAL EQUITY
The HTQ is eligible to earn:
5% equity in Territorial Tutoring SA (Pty) Ltd
5.2 VESTING STRUCTURE
Equity is not granted upfront. It vests as follows:
2% equity upon formal appointment as HTQ
Remaining 3% equity vests over 12 months, as follows:
0.25% per month, for each full month of:
maintained system integrity
no systemic quality collapse
5.3 VESTING CONDITIONS
Ongoing vesting requires:
Sustained system-wide quality integrity
No systemic breakdown across pods
Continued ability to operate independently
Successful scaling of TD layer

If system quality degrades:
Vesting may be paused, reduced, or revoked
Vesting resumes only once:
standards are fully restored
stability is re-established
No retroactive vesting applies for paused periods.
5.4 FORFEITURE
If the HTQ:
Resigns prematurely
Is removed due to performance failure
Breaches system integrity
Unvested equity is forfeited immediately.
6. NO CONTROL RIGHTS
Equity granted does not automatically confer:
Board control
Operational override of CEO
Strategic decision authority
All governance remains subject to company structure.
7. TERMINATION
TT may remove the HTQ at any time in cases including:
System quality degradation
Failure to enforce standards
Loss of operational independence
Breach of agreements
8. CONFIDENTIALITY & SYSTEM PROTECTION
All prior confidentiality and system protection obligations remain fully enforceable.
9. FINAL AUTHORITY
The HTQ acknowledges:

The system belongs to TT
Authority is granted based on performance
Continuation in role is conditional on maintaining system integrity
10. ACKNOWLEDGEMENT
The HTQ confirms:
Full understanding of role responsibility
Acceptance of performance-based authority
Alignment with system-first principles
