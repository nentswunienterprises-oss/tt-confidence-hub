TT-TDA-001 - TERRITORY DIRECTOR CONTRACTOR
AGREEMENT
This Agreement is entered into between:
Territorial Tutoring SA (Pty) Ltd (“TT”)
and
______________________________________ (“Territory Director”)
Effective Date: ______________________
1. NATURE OF RELATIONSHIP
The Territory Director is engaged as an independent contractor.
This Agreement does not constitute employment.
The Territory Director is not entitled to:
salary guarantees
employee benefits
fixed working hours
All compensation is performance-based.
2. ROLE DEFINITION
The Territory Director is responsible for enforcing the TT-OS system within assigned
pods.
This includes:
Auditing tutor sessions where applicable
Monitoring tutor execution quality
Identifying breakdown patterns
Enforcing corrective actions
Maintaining system integrity across all assigned tutors
The Territory Director does not:
redesign systems
modify TT-OS
operate outside defined structures
3. COMPENSATION
The Territory Director earns:

25% of platform revenue per assigned pod
Example:
Pod generates R9,000 → TD earns R2,250
Compensation is dependent on:
Active students
Pod activity
Continued assignment
No payment is due for:
inactive pods
removed assignments
system breaches
4. ASSIGNMENT & CONTROL
Pods are assigned and may be reassigned at TT’s discretion
TT may increase or decrease pod allocation at any time
No permanent claim exists over any pod
5. PERFORMANCE STANDARD
The Territory Director is expected to:
Enforce TT-OS strictly and consistently
Maintain high tutor execution quality
Detect and act on system drift immediately
Operate independently without reliance on escalation
Failure to meet these standards may result in removal.
6. TERMINATION
TT may terminate this Agreement at any time, with or without notice, in cases
including but not limited to:
Failure to enforce system standards
Poor performance
Inconsistent auditing
Breach of TT protocols
The Territory Director may also terminate this Agreement with written notice.

7. NO EQUITY OR OWNERSHIP
This Agreement does not grant:
shares in TT
profit participation beyond defined compensation
ownership rights
Any future equity consideration is separate and not implied.
8. CONFIDENTIALITY
The Territory Director agrees to keep confidential:
TT-OS system
training frameworks
internal processes
student and tutor data
This obligation continues after termination.
9. NON-COMPETE / NON-CIRCUMVENTION
During the term of this Agreement and for a reasonable period thereafter, the
Territory Director shall not:
replicate TT systems
directly solicit TT students or tutors
create or participate in competing tutoring systems using TT methodologies
10. GENERAL
This Agreement constitutes the full understanding between the parties and may only
be amended in writing.
