This Declaration is entered into between:
Territorial Tutoring SA (Pty) Ltd (“TT”)
and
________________________________________ (“Territory Director”)
Effective Date: ______________________
1. PURPOSE
This Declaration governs the integrity, accuracy, and honesty of all audit activity
conducted by the Territory Director.
Auditing is not a support function.
It is a truth-reporting function.
2. TRUTH OVER RELATIONSHIPS
The Territory Director agrees:
Personal relationships with tutors are irrelevant to audit outcomes
Discomfort in reporting issues is not a valid reason for omission
No tutor is to be protected from accurate evaluation
All audits must reflect actual execution reality, not perceived effort or intention.
3. ACCURATE REPORTING OBLIGATION
The Territory Director must:
Report all observed deviations from TT-OS
Record breakdown patterns exactly as they occur
Avoid exaggeration, minimization, or distortion
Audits must reflect:
what happened, not what was intended
4. ZERO OMISSION RULE
The Territory Director shall not:
Ignore known violations
Delay reporting to avoid conflict
Selectively report only favourable observations

Failure to report known issues constitutes:
intentional concealment
5. AUDIT CONSISTENCY
The Territory Director must:
Apply the same standards across all tutors
Avoid bias, favouritism, or selective strictness
Maintain consistency across pods
6. FALSE REPORTING
The following are considered breaches:
Reporting compliance where non-compliance exists
Downplaying severity of execution breakdowns
Submitting incomplete or misleading audit data
False reporting undermines system integrity and may result in immediate removal.
7. ACCOUNTABILITY
The Territory Director acknowledges:
Audits may be reviewed, cross-checked, and challenged
Audit accuracy is a measurable performance metric
Repeated inaccuracies indicate unfitness for the role
8. SYSTEM FIRST PRINCIPLE
The Territory Director confirms:
The system must be protected, even if individuals are affected
Enforcement decisions must prioritize TT integrity over personal dynamics
9. BREACH CONSEQUENCES
Any of the following may result in immediate removal:
Intentional omission of violations
Repeated inaccurate audits
Bias-driven reporting
Concealment of system drift
No warning period is required for integrity breaches.

10. DECLARATION
The Territory Director confirms:
Commitment to objective, accurate, and complete audit reporting
Willingness to enforce truth regardless of discomfort
Understanding that audit integrity is non-negotiable
