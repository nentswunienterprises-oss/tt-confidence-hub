1. PURPOSE
This Addendum places the Territory Director on a performance evaluation track for
potential transition into the role of:
Head of Training & Quality (HTQ)
This is not a promotion pathway.
This is a selection filter based on proven system capability.
2. NO GUARANTEE OF PROMOTION
The Territory Director acknowledges:
Inclusion in the HTQ track does not guarantee selection
No entitlement to the HTQ role exists
No expectation of equity or additional compensation is created
Selection is determined solely by measured performance outcomes.
3. EVALUATION PERIOD
The Territory Director will be evaluated over a defined period of:
8-12 weeks (minimum)
During this time:
Performance is continuously assessed
No early promotion decisions will be made
4. CORE EVALUATION CRITERIA
The Territory Director will be assessed on the following:

4.1 TUTOR EXECUTION QUALITY (PRIMARY)
Tutors consistently execute TT-OS correctly
Breakdown patterns reduce over time
Students demonstrate increasing stability
This is measured through actual tutor output, not reported activity.
4.2 SYSTEM DRIFT CONTROL
Frequency of drift within assigned pods
Speed and effectiveness of corrective action
Drift that persists indicates failure.
4.3 AUDIT ACCURACY
Accuracy of reported audits when cross-checked
Ability to detect subtle violations
Missed or incorrect audits reduce eligibility.
4.4 TD REPLICATION CAPABILITY
Ability to train and develop new TDs
Performance quality of trained TDs
Minimum requirement:
Successful development of 2-3 TDs
4.5 OPERATIONAL INDEPENDENCE
Ability to operate without escalation
Minimal reliance on founder or executive team intervention
Dependence indicates non-readiness.
5. MINIMUM QUALIFICATION THRESHOLDS
To be considered for HTQ, the Territory Director must:
Successfully manage at least 3 pods
Maintain high tutor execution quality across pods
Demonstrate no systemic quality collapse
Train 2-3 TDs successfully
Operate independently without supervision

Failure in any of the above disqualifies eligibility.
6. SELECTION PROCESS
All candidates are evaluated concurrently
Performance is ranked comparatively
Only one candidate will be selected
TT reserves full discretion in final selection.
7. POST-SELECTION OUTCOME
Upon selection:
The candidate will be formally appointed as Head of Training & Quality (HTQ)
A separate HTQ Agreement will be executed
Compensation and equity structures will be activated under new terms
All non-selected candidates:
Remain Territory Directors
Continue under existing agreements
Have no claim to HTQ role or equity
8. NO RETROSPECTIVE CLAIMS
The Territory Director agrees:
No claims may be made based on perceived performance
Selection decisions are final and non-negotiable
9. ACKNOWLEDGEMENT
The Territory Director confirms:
Full understanding of evaluation criteria
Acceptance of performance-based selection
Willingness to be measured objectively
