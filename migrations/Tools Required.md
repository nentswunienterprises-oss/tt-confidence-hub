The Gooseneck Phone Holder Approach

![alt text](<../../../Pictures/Territorial Tutoring/61fV80l97NL.webp>)

Unlike traditional online tutoring that relies on digital whiteboards and complex tools, **TT focuses on a hands-on, practical approach.**

 **How It Works:**

- Tutors use their **smartphone camera, positioned on a gooseneck phone holder**, to show their work from a top-down view.
- This **mimics the student's perspective**, making it easier for them to **follow step-by-step solutions** and replicate the problem-solving process.
- The method eliminates unnecessary distractions, allowing students to **focus solely on understanding math concepts.**

 **Advantages:**

- **Easy for students to follow** (mirrors a classroom-like setup)
- **No need for expensive software or gadgets**
- **Fast and efficient teaching style**
- **Builds a strong student-tutor connection**
