# MPLEM Strategy -Pod Adoption Scenarios & Net Profit Calculation

## MPLEM Strategy Summary

- Each pod serves 36 students (buyers/parents).
- The first pod (Foundation Pod) covers all central company operating costs. Net profit: **R36,000** (9 months).
- Every additional pod (Financed Pod) runs at full yield. Net profit: **R73,224** (9 months).
- Total Net Profit for any wave:

  **Total Net Profit = Foundation Pod Net + (Number of Pods − 1) × Financed Pod Net**

  Where:
  - Foundation Pod Net = R36,000
  - Financed Pod Net = R73,224
  - Number of Pods = Total pods in the wave

## Net Profit Calculation for Each Scenario

### 1. 30,000 Buyers (834 Pods)
- Foundation Pod Net: R36,000
- Financed Pods: 833 × R73,224 = R60,978,192
- **Total Net Profit:** R36,000 + R60,978,192 = **R61,014,192**

### 2. 60,000 Buyers (1,667 Pods)
- Foundation Pod Net: R36,000
- Financed Pods: 1,666 × R73,224 = R121,998,784
- **Total Net Profit:** R36,000 + R121,998,784 = **R122,034,784**

### 3. 100,000 Buyers (2,778 Pods)
- Foundation Pod Net: R36,000
- Financed Pods: 2,777 × R73,224 = R203,420,248
- **Total Net Profit:** R36,000 + R203,420,248 = **R203,456,248**

---

## Simple Calculation Steps

1. Divide the number of buyers by 36 to get pods needed (round up if not whole number).
2. Use the formula:

   **Total Net Profit = R36,000 + (Pods Needed − 1) × R73,224**

3. This gives the total net profit for a 9-month cycle at any adoption level.
