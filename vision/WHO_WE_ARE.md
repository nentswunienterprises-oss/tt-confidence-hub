# WHO WE ARE

**Response Integrity is a calm-performance academic support brand for Grades 6-9 learners.**

## What we do

- Train learners to respond better when mathematics becomes difficult.
- Deliver a repeatable operating system, Response Integrity-OS, that builds calm execution through clarity, repetition, and phase-aware development.
- Keep the public brand parent-safe while the operational layer stays precise.
- Run the current live tutor validation track with matric-completed or young-adult candidates.

## Our promise

We do not sell motivation, quick fixes, or last-minute rescue.

We build the conditions where calm execution becomes more stable through direct attention, structured repetition, and consistent cadence.

## Non-negotiables

- Tone: calm, precise, observational. Short lines, no hype.
- Product: system first, personality second. Tutors execute the system; they do not freestyle the doctrine.
- Doctrine: sessions follow the phase. Model -> Apply -> Guide is most visible in Clarity work, not every phase of the operating system.
- Measurement: behaviour change under pressure is the primary metric. Marks follow that stability.

## Current reality

- Public audience: Grades 6-9
- Current tutor validation track: matric-completed or young-adult candidates
- Future high-school leadership track: later
- Primary-school referral pilot: available now
