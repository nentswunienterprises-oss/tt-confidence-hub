# Index Questions

What you need to know about your repository at a glance.

## Repo Map

- **What are the major folder/module divisions in your codebase?**
- **Where is the frontend code located, and what framework/language does it use?**
- **Where is the backend code located, and what runtime/language does it use?**
- **What are the critical utility or script directories?**
- **Where are migrations or database-related files stored?**
- **What is the purpose of the local brain/context folder?**
- **How often does the repo structure change, and who owns keeping this map updated?**

## Repo Snapshot

- **What is the current complete file inventory of the repository?**
- **How many files are in each major folder?**
- **What are the largest files, and why do they exist?**
- **Are there files that should not be tracked (built artifacts, node_modules, dist/)?**
- **What is the total size of the repository, and is it growing or stable?**
- **How frequently should the snapshot be regenerated?**
- **What build or generated files appear in the snapshot that should be ignored?**
