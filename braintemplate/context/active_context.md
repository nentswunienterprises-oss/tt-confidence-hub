# Brain Context -Current State & Decisions

Answer these questions to capture the current state, known problems, and active decision-making context for your project.

## 1. Project Identity

- **What is the official name and purpose of this project?**
- **Who are the primary users/stakeholders?**
- **What problem does it solve?**
- **In one sentence, what is the core mission?**

## 2. Current Development Phase

- **What stage is the project in?** (concept, alpha, beta, production, mature, legacy)
- **What are the top 3 priorities right now?**
- **What is blocked or in progress?**
- **What was completed in the last sprint/cycle?**

## 3. Known Technical Debt

- **What parts of the codebase are fragile or need refactoring?**
- **Are there deprecated dependencies or outdated patterns still in use?**
- **What architectural compromises exist and why?**
- **What quick wins would improve code quality?**

## 4. Active Decisions / In‑Flight Work

- **What major decisions are pending?** (technology choices, architectural changes, feature scope)
- **Who is responsible for each decision?**
- **What is the deadline or timeline?**
- **What information is still needed to decide?**

## 5. Recent Changes & Their Impact

- **What was added, removed, or changed in the last release?**
- **Were there any breaking changes?**
- **What bugs or regressions were introduced?**
- **What feedback did you get from users?**

## 6. Known Risks & Constraints

- **What could break this project?** (external dependencies, scaling limits, team capacity)
- **Are there regulatory, legal, or compliance constraints?**
- **What performance limits exist?**
- **Are there known security vulnerabilities?**

## 7. Integration Points

- **What external systems or APIs does this project depend on?**
- **What other projects integrate with this one?**
- **Are there shared databases or resources?**
- **What SLAs or uptime requirements exist?**

## 8. Team Context

- **Who owns this project?**
- **Who are the key contributors?**
- **What expertise gaps exist?**
- **When was the last knowledge transfer or review?**

## 9. Active Questions / Open Threads

- **What uncertainty remains about the direction?**
- **What assumptions are we making that could be wrong?**
- **What would you ask an expert if you had the chance?**
- **What needs clarification before moving forward?**

## 10. Success Metrics

- **How do you measure success for this project?**
- **What would constitute failure?**
- **Are there SLAs, uptime targets, or performance benchmarks?**
- **How often are metrics reviewed?**
