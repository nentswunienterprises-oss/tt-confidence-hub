# Vision Questions

What you are building and why it matters.

## Identity & Positioning

- **In one sentence, what is your institution or product?**
- **What do you actually do (not what you claim, what you deliver)?**
- **What is your core promise to users or stakeholders?**
- **What are the non-negotiable principles or values?**
  - How do you speak about your work?
  - What is required in your product or service?
  - How do you measure success internally?
- **What is your positioning line for public communication?**
- **Who is your primary audience?**
- **Who is explicitly not your audience?**

## Mission & Operations

- **What problem are you solving, and for whom?**
- **How do you solve it (what is your operating system or methodology)?**
- **What are your core operational principles?**
- **Who do you serve (students, parents, teachers, partners)?**
- **What does each stakeholder receive from your system?**
- **What does each stakeholder contribute to your system?**
- **How is success measured with each stakeholder?**
- **What is the structure of your pilot or launch model?**
- **What are your primary metrics for growth or impact?**
- **What product tiers or offerings do you provide?**

## Brand & Tone

- **What is your core positioning?**
- **What tone or voice defines your communication?**
- **What words or phrases do you use?**
- **What words or phrases do you absolutely avoid?**
- **What is your visual or aesthetic identity?**
- **How should your work feel to the audience?**
- **What imagery or metaphors represent your brand?**
- **How do you earn trust without hype?**

## Business Model & Scaling

- **What is your revenue model?**
- **What is the financial structure of your primary offering?**
- **How do you scale without increasing costs?**
- **What is the unit economics (per student, per tutor, per pod)?**
- **How do you replicate your system?**
- **What are the fixed costs, and what are the variable costs?**
- **What is your profit per unit or per cycle?**
- **How do you fund growth without external capital?**
- **What milestones define success at different scales?**

## Principles & Culture

- **What are the five pillars or foundations of your institution?**
- **What does each pillar represent and why is it essential?**
- **What is your cultural identity or ethos?**
- **How do you select and develop talent?**
- **What is the relationship between leadership and operations?**
- **How do you maintain standards while scaling?**
- **What are the non-negotiable rules for technology or systems?**
- **How do you stay accountable to your mission?**

## Constitutional Rules

- **What are the inviolable rules that govern your system?**
- **What cannot be compromised for growth or efficiency?**
- **How do you protect against mission drift?**
- **What constraints protect users or stakeholders?**
- **How do you handle conflicts between growth and principle?**
- **What decisions require explicit human approval?**
- **How do you audit or measure adherence to principles?**
