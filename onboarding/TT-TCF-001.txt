SECTION B: PROGRAMME DEFINITION AND ROLE
By accepting this form, the Contractor acknowledges and accepts the following:
Response Integrity is not a tutoring service.
It is a response-conditioning system delivered through mathematics.
The purpose of every session is to train how a learner responds under difficulty, not to provide casual homework help or panic rescue.
The Contractor operates as a response-conditioning operator responsible for stabilizing learner execution under pressure.
Every session is executed under the phase-aware Response Integrity Operating System (Response Integrity-OS).
The active phase determines how much modeling, guidance, difficulty, and time pressure are permitted.
Clarity:
Model -> Apply -> Guide is most visible here.
Structured Execution:
Independent starts, step order, and repeatability are protected here.
Controlled Discomfort:
Difficulty is held without rescue.
Time Pressure Stability:
Structure is tested under urgency.
The Contractor does not merely fix answers.
The Contractor helps train how the learner behaves when answers become unclear.
