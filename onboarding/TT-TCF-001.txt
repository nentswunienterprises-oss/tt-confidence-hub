SECTION B: PROGRAMME DEFINITION AND ROLE
By accepting this form, the Contractor acknowledges and accepts the following:
Territorial Tutoring is not a tutoring service.
It is a response-conditioning system delivered through mathematics.
The purpose of every session is to train how a learner responds under difficulty, not to explain content or assist with homework.
The Contractor operates as a response-conditioning operator, responsible for stabilizing learner execution under pressure.
Every session is executed strictly under the TT Operating System (TT-OS):
Model -> Apply -> Guide
Model:
Demonstrate structured, calm execution
Apply:
Require independent learner attempt under friction
Guide:
Stabilize response - not rescue, not spoon-feed
The Contractor does not fix answers.
The Contractor fixes how the learner behaves when answers are unclear.
