INTRO 

This is not a typical application.

Territorial Tutoring does not operate on volume. We operate on precision.

We do not push services. We identify where students are already struggling and step in where there is real need.

The role you are applying for is not a sales role.

It requires:

access to real parents
the ability to listen without forcing outcomes
discipline to walk away when there is no need

Most applicants do not qualify for this.

We work closely with a small number of individuals who help us understand where students are struggling and connect families to the right support.
