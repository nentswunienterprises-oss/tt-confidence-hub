**Response Integrity-EGP-002 - NON-CIRCUMVENTION & NON-SOLICITATION AGREEMENT**

This Agreement is entered into between:

**Territorial Tutoring SA (Pty) Ltd** (“Response Integrity”)
and
**[Full Name]** (“Education Growth Partner” / “EGP”)

Effective Date: ______________________

---

## 1. PURPOSE

This Agreement protects Response Integrity’s:

* client relationships
* tutor network
* operational structure

The EGP acknowledges that all relationships introduced or accessed through Response Integrity remain the exclusive property of Response Integrity.

---

## 2. NON-CIRCUMVENTION

The EGP shall not, directly or indirectly:

* Engage Response Integrity clients outside the Response Integrity system
* Offer tutoring or related services privately to Response Integrity clients
* Redirect parents or students away from Response Integrity
* Facilitate third-party services to Response Integrity clients

All clients introduced remain within Response Integrity.

---

## 3. NON-SOLICITATION (CLIENTS)

The EGP shall not:

* Contact Response Integrity parents for personal or external services
* AResponse Integrityempt to move Response Integrity clients to another platform
* Re-engage former Response Integrity clients outside Response Integrity

This applies during and after the relationship with Response Integrity.

---

## 4. NON-SOLICITATION (TUTORS)

The EGP shall not:

* Recruit or aResponse Integrityempt to recruit Response Integrity tutors
* Engage Response Integrity tutors for external or private services
* Encourage tutors to leave Response Integrity

---

## 5. DURATION

These restrictions apply:

* During the term of engagement
* For **24 months after termination**

---

## 6. BREACH

Any breach of this Agreement constitutes:

* Immediate termination
* Forfeiture of all unpaid commissions
* Potential legal action

---

## 7. NO WORKAROUNDS

The EGP shall not:

* Use intermediaries
* Act through third parties
* Indirectly bypass Response Integrity

Circumvention through any means is treated as direct breach.

---

## 8. ACKNOWLEDGEMENT

The EGP confirms:

* Understanding of Response Integrity ownership of relationships
* Agreement not to exploit access gained through Response Integrity
* Acceptance of strict non-circumvention enforcement
