**TT-EGP-003 - CONFIDENTIALITY & SYSTEM PROTECTION AGREEMENT**

This Agreement is entered into between:

**Territorial Tutoring SA (Pty) Ltd** (“TT”)
and
**[Full Name]** (“Education Growth Partner” / “EGP”)

Effective Date: ______________________

---

## 1. PURPOSE

This Agreement protects TT’s proprietary systems, methodologies, and internal structures.

The EGP acknowledges that TT operates using a defined system (TT-OS) and strategic framework that is confidential and commercially sensitive.

---

## 2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to:

* TT-OS (Territorial Tutoring Operating System)
* Training methods and drill structures
* Intake and diagnostic processes
* Pricing models and revenue structures
* Internal documents, agreements, and frameworks
* Tutor management systems and quality control processes
* Strategic positioning and messaging

---

## 3. NON-DISCLOSURE

The EGP shall not:

* Share confidential information with any third party
* Disclose TT systems or processes externally
* Discuss internal operations outside TT

---

## 4. NON-REPRODUCTION

The EGP shall not:

* Copy or replicate TT systems
* Recreate TT-OS in any form
* Train or assist others using TT methodologies

---

## 5. LIMITED USE

The EGP may only use information provided:

  for the purpose of fulfilling their role within TT

No other use is permitted.

---

## 6. DATA PROTECTION

The EGP shall:

* Protect parent and student information
* Not store, export, or misuse client data
* Not retain data after termination

---

## 7. BREACH

Any breach of confidentiality constitutes:

* Immediate termination
* Forfeiture of all unpaid commissions
* Potential legal action

---

## 8. DURATION

Confidentiality obligations apply:

indefinitely, including after termination

---

## 9. ACKNOWLEDGEMENT

The EGP confirms:

* Understanding of TT’s proprietary nature
* Agreement to protect all confidential systems
* Acceptance of strict confidentiality enforcement
