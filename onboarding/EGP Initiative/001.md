**TT-EGP-001 - EDUCATION GROWTH PARTNER AGREEMENT**

This Agreement is entered into between:

**Territorial Tutoring SA (Pty) Ltd** (“TT”)
and
**[Full Name]** (“Education Growth Partner” / “EGP”)

Effective Date: ______________________

---

## 1. PURPOSE

This Agreement governs the role of the Education Growth Partner as a **distribution operator responsible for sourcing qualified students** into the TT system.

The EGP is not an employee, advisor, or decision-maker.
The EGP is a **performance-based acquisition operator**.

---

## 2. ROLE DEFINITION

The EGP is responsible for:

* Identifying parents whose children demonstrate real academic performance breakdown
* Introducing qualified parents to TT
* Facilitating enrollment into the TT system

The EGP shall not:

* Teach or tutor students
* Modify or interpret TT services
* Make guarantees regarding results
* Interfere with TT-OS or tutor operations

---

## 3. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

The EGP operates as an independent contractor.

The EGP is not entitled to:

* Salary
* Benefits
* Employment protections

No employer-employee relationship is created.

---

## 4. COMMISSION STRUCTURE (PHASE 1 -POD ACTIVATION)

The EGP earns commission only under the following conditions:

### 4.1 Activation Payment

* **R1,500** is paid upon successful contribution to a fully filled pod

### 4.2 Retention Payment

* **R750** is paid only if the student remains active for **30 days**

---

### 4.3 PAYMENT CONDITIONS

Commission is earned only when:

* The student is accepted by TT
* The parent completes payment
* The student remains active (for retention payout)

No payment is made for:

* leads
* inquiries
* incomplete enrollments

---

## 5. POD-BASED INCENTIVE STRUCTURE

Commission is tied to:

👉 **fully activated pods**, not individual student activity

Partial contribution does not guarantee full payout.

---

## 6. NO ENTITLEMENT

The EGP acknowledges:

* No equity is granted
* No ownership stake is created
* No future claims may be made

---

## 7. TERRITORY ASSIGNMENT

The EGP may be assigned specific:

* Schools
* Communities
* Networks

The EGP shall not:

* Operate outside assigned territory without approval
* Interfere with other EGPs

---

## 8. REPRESENTATION LIMITATIONS

The EGP shall not:

* Misrepresent TT services
* Promise guaranteed results
* Provide academic advice beyond defined positioning

All communication must align with TT positioning.

---

## 9. TERMINATION

TT may terminate this Agreement at any time:

* Without cause
* Without notice
* Without future obligation

Unmet conditions result in no payout.

---

## 10. CONFIDENTIALITY & SYSTEM PROTECTION

The EGP agrees not to:

* Share TT systems or processes
* Disclose internal structures
* Replicate TT model externally

---

## 11. ACKNOWLEDGEMENT

The EGP confirms:

* Understanding of performance-based nature
* Acceptance of zero entitlement structure
* Alignment with TT operational boundaries
