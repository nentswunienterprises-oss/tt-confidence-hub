**Response Integrity-EGP-004 - REPRESENTATION & CONDUCT AGREEMENT**

This Agreement is entered into between:

**Territorial Tutoring SA (Pty) Ltd** (“Response Integrity”)
and
**[Full Name]** (“Education Growth Partner” / “EGP”)

Effective Date: ______________________

---

## 1. PURPOSE

This Agreement governs how the EGP represents Response Integrity in all external interactions.

The EGP is not authorized to interpret, modify, or expand Response Integrity’s positioning.

The EGP must communicate **only within defined boundaries**.

---

## 2. ROLE IN COMMUNICATION

The EGP is responsible for:

* Identifying potential need
* Introducing parents to Response Integrity
* Positioning Response Integrity accurately

The EGP is not responsible for:

* Explaining technical training methods
* Diagnosing academic issues in detail
* Closing complex decisions

---

## 3. POSITIONING RULE (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

The EGP must represent Response Integrity as:

A system that helps students who **break under pressure in math**, despite understanding the work

The EGP shall not position Response Integrity as:

* “extra lessons”
* “homework help”
* “exam tips”
* “guaranteed marks improvement”

---

## 4. NO FALSE PROMISES

The EGP shall not:

* Guarantee results
* Promise specific marks or outcomes
* Overstate Response Integrity capabilities

All outcomes depend on system execution.

---

## 5. NO PRESSURE SELLING

The EGP shall not:

* Pressure parents into signing up
* Create artificial urgency
* Mislead parents about availability or results

If need is not present:

The EGP must disengage

---

## 6. NEED-FIRST RULE (POWER LISTENING)

The EGP must:

* Identify whether a real academic breakdown exists
* Engage only where need is clear

If need is absent:

The EGP must not push enrollment

---

## 7. COMMUNICATION STANDARDS

The EGP shall:

* Speak clearly and accurately
* Avoid exaggeration
* Avoid informal or unprofessional messaging

All communication must reflect Response Integrity’s standards.

---

## 8. SOCIAL MEDIA & PUBLIC REPRESENTATION

The EGP shall not:

* Post misleading content about Response Integrity
* Represent themselves as a tutor or expert
* Create independent Response Integrity-branded material

---

## 9. BREACH

Any breach of this Agreement may result in:

* Immediate termination
* Forfeiture of unpaid commissions
* Removal from Response Integrity network

---

## 10. ACKNOWLEDGEMENT

The EGP confirms:

* Understanding of representation boundaries
* Agreement to communicate within defined positioning
* Commitment to protecting Response Integrity’s brand integrity
* Commitment to engage only where need is clear and genuine
